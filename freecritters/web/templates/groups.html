You can join and form groups with other users here. Groups have many fun features available to their members, including their own private forums. There are three types of groups:

  • Clubs, whose members can join guilds and other clubs.
  • Guilds, whose members can join clubs, but not other guilds.
  • Cults, whose members can be in no other group.
